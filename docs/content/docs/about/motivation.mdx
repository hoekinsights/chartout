ChartOut was started by me, [Mattijn van Hoek](https://github.com/mattijn). I am a maintainer of [Vega-Altair](https://altair-viz.github.io) and steering committee member of the [Vega](https://vega.github.io) ecosystem, which I do as a leisure activity predominantly in my free time. I stepped up as a maintainer when Vega-Altair seemed to be getting stalled, and it has been a big part of my open source life since.

![NumFOCUS Project Summit 2024](/numfocus_projectsummit24.jpg)

<figcaption>Taken at NumFOCUS Project Summit 2024 in Boston, USA</figcaption>

Open source maintainers often do this work by giving their free time to things they care about. As the complexity of a project grows, that becomes harder to sustain. That is why we as maintainers of the Vega projects applied to become a member of [NumFOCUS](https://numfocus.org), a nonprofit that helps open source projects become more sustainable.

ChartOut is my personal attempt to support that same goal in a different way. The idea is simple. Users get something nice, a physical product made from their own chart or data visualization, and by buying it they also support the open source tools they use every day. ChartOut donates 10% of every purchase back to NumFOCUS.

It is a loop where buying something pays back to the system that made it possible.
