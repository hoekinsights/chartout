ChartOut is built on a simple idea. Well crafted data visualizations deserve a place beyond the screen. They can summarize months of research, communicate an important insight, commemorate a milestone, or simply be a chart you are proud of. ChartOut makes it easy to turn them into physical products without leaving your existing workflow.

This idea comes from how data visualizations are created today. Most charts are made in open source tools like Vega Altair and Vega, maintained by communities that often work in their spare time. These tools are used across research, journalism, education, and industry, but the outputs they produce almost always remain digital, living in papers, dashboards, and presentations.

I am Mattijn van Hoek, a maintainer of Vega Altair and a member of the Vega steering committee. I stepped up as a maintainer when Vega Altair risked becoming inactive, and contributing to it has been a significant part of my work, mostly in my free time.

![NumFOCUS Project Summit 2024](/numfocus_projectsummit24.jpg)

<figcaption>Taken at NumFOCUS Project Summit 2024 in Boston, USA</figcaption>

Most open source maintainers contribute in their spare time. As projects grow in scope and complexity, that model becomes increasingly difficult to sustain. That is one reason the Vega maintainers joined NumFOCUS as a sponsored project, to help build a more sustainable future for the ecosystem.

ChartOut is my own attempt to support that same goal in a different way. Users receive a physical product made from their own data visualization, and each purchase also supports the ecosystem behind it. ChartOut donates 10 percent of every purchase to NumFOCUS.

Every purchase gives something back to the ecosystem that made it possible.
