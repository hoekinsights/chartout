ChartOut adds a print store to your notebook or web application.

Pass it a chart, and ChartOut opens an embedded store where users can preview their visualization on real products, adjust its placement, and complete their purchase. Products are printed on demand and shipped worldwide.

<Journey />

## Works with popular chart libraries

ChartOut is designed to support a wide range of visualization libraries across Python and JavaScript.

### Python

- Vega-Altair
- Matplotlib

Planned support includes vgplot, pyobsplot, and Plotly.

### JavaScript

- Observable Plot
- ECharts

Libraries that render SVG output may also work by passing the SVG element to the `openWith*` helpers.

## Flexible integration

- Let users browse and select a product.
- Preselect a product before opening the store.
- Build an entire shopping cart ahead of time.

## Interactive 3D previews

Users can preview their chart on realistic 3D product models before purchasing, with fine-grained controls for positioning and scaling.

## Worldwide fulfillment

Products are printed on demand and shipped to most countries worldwide.
