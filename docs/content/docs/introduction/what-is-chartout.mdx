---
title: What is ChartOut?
---

**ChartOut** converts data visualizations into print ready products such as mugs, canvases, posters, and mousepads. Pass it a chart, and the embedded widget lets users preview it on a realistic 3D product, adjust its placement, and complete their purchase.

## Highlights

### 1. Works with any chart library

Compatible with Altair, matplotlib, Plotly, pyobsplot, Observable Plot, D3, ECharts, and any other library. If your charts can be rendered as an image or SVG, ChartOut can turn them into products.

### 2. Flexible integration patterns

Let users choose a product, preselect one for them, or build the entire shopping cart ahead of time.

### 3. Real time 3D preview

Interactive 3D product previews with fine grained placement controls.

### 4. Ships worldwide

Products are printed on demand and delivered to most countries worldwide.
