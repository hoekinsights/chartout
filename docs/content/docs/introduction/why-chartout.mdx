---
title: Why ChartOut
---

ChartOut exists for two reasons.

### 1. Your work deserves a place beyond the screen

Well-crafted data visualizations deserve a place beyond the screen. They can summarize months of research, communicate an important insight, commemorate a milestone, or simply be a chart you are proud of. The challenge for me was to see if this could happen inside the software data scientists and domain specialists already use, mostly notebooks. Could a notebook cell become an app of its own? ChartOut makes it easy to turn a chart into a physical product without leaving your existing workflow.

### 2. Giving back to the communities behind it

Most charts are made with open source tools maintained by communities, often in their spare time or alongside paid work. I want to find a way to give back to those communities. Therefore, each purchase supports the ecosystem behind it. ChartOut donates 10 percent of every purchase to NumFOCUS. A chart from a notebook or embedded app can become a product someone can hold. Whether the author orders it or someone else does, that purchase supports the communities behind the tools.

## About me

I am Mattijn van Hoek, an open source maintainer myself (Vega project). I see it as a privilege to contribute alongside people who care deeply about similar things. For me, most of that work happens in my spare time, and there is space to think things through without the pressure of a typical workday.
