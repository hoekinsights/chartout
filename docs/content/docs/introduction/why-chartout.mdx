title: Why ChartOut

---

ChartOut exists for two reasons.

### 1. Your work deserves a place beyond the screen

Well crafted data visualizations deserve a place beyond the screen. They can summarize months of research, communicate an important insight, commemorate a milestone, or simply be a chart you are proud of. The challenge for me was to see if this could happen inside the software data scientists and domain specialists already use, mostly notebooks. Could a notebook cell become an app of its own? ChartOut makes it easy to turn a chart into a physical product without leaving your existing workflow.

### 2. Giving back to the communities behind it

Most charts are made in open source tools, maintained by communities that often work in their spare time. I want to find a way to give back to those communities, who build software that means a lot to the people using it. Each purchase supports the ecosystem behind it. ChartOut donates 10 percent of every purchase to NumFOCUS. You do your work in your notebook, you turn it into a product that you or others around you can appreciate, and by doing that, you give back to the communities you are building on.

## About me

I am Mattijn van Hoek, and I am an open source maintainer myself (Vega). It is an honor to work on software that so many people use, and you get a lot back in return. You learn from people who care about similar things, and you can build on each other's work.
