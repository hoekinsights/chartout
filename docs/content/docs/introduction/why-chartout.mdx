---
title: Why ChartOut
---

ChartOut exists for two reasons.

### 1. Your work deserves a place beyond the screen

Well-crafted data visualizations deserve a place beyond the screen. They can summarize months of research, communicate an important insight, commemorate a milestone, or simply be a chart you're proud of.

I wanted to see whether printing those charts could become part of the tools people already use, especially notebooks. Could a notebook cell become an application of its own? Could someone preview, customize, and order a printed chart without leaving their workflow?

ChartOut is my answer to those questions. It lets people turn charts into physical products directly from their notebook or web application.

### 2. Giving back to the communities behind it

Most charts are created with open-source software maintained by communities of volunteers and contributors, often alongside their day jobs. ChartOut is also built on that ecosystem, and I wanted it to give something back.

With every purchase, ChartOut donates to NumFOCUS to support the open-source projects behind the charts people create. A visualization can become something you keep on your desk, hang on a wall, or give as a gift and every purchase also helps sustain the software that made it possible.

## About me

I'm Mattijn van Hoek, an open-source maintainer and contributor to the [Vega project](https://vega.github.io). 

Contributing to open source has introduced me to thoughtful, generous people who care deeply about building good software. Most of my own work happens in my spare time, where there's room to experiment, explore ideas, and build projects like ChartOut.
